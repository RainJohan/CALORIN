⛩ How to Play
I
Select a Sorcerer from the shop at the top. Each costs Cursed Energy (✦ CE).
II
Click any grid cell to deploy them on that lane. Block Curses before they reach the barrier.
III
Collect CE orbs (✦) by clicking them — they fall from the sky and from Shoko. Don't let them expire!
IV
Survive all 5 Curse Waves. Higher waves spawn stronger Special Grade curses — plan your placement.
V
Sukuna (💀) detonates after 3 seconds, annihilating all curses in range — use him wisely.
