⛩ How to Play
Sorcerer's Field Manual
I
✦
Collect Cursed Energy (✦ CE)
CE orbs fall from the sky and are produced by Shoko. Click them immediately — they expire quickly. CE is your only currency for deploying sorcerers.
II
👁
Select a Sorcerer from the Shop
Click any card in the shop at the top to select it. Each sorcerer has a unique CE cost and ability. The card glows gold when selected.
III
⛩
Deploy on the Grid
Click any empty grid cell to place your sorcerer. They auto-attack curses in their lane. Use 🗡 Remove (free) to reposition a sorcerer anytime.
IV
🏯
Defend the Barrier — 5 Waves
Curses march right to left. If one crosses the left edge the Barrier loses a life. Lose all 5 lives and it falls. Later waves spawn Finger Bearers and Special Grades.
V
💀
Sukuna's Malevolent Shrine
💀 Sukuna detonates 3 seconds after placement with a devastating AoE radius of 2.4 cells. One use only — save him for dense waves or a Special Grade ambush.
